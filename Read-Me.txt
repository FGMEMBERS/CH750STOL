This is the Zenith Aircraft CH750 STOL, a light utility STOL aircraft made for two persons.
The superb 3d-model has been ported from the CH750 for x-plane by hydroz.net who has kindly agreed to release it under GPL. You can get the X-plane version of this and many other superb aircraft on hydroz.net
The aircraft, respectively FDM, nasal, sounds and effects are based on the P92 by helijah et al.
